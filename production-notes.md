Two of the archive.com texts that this was proofed against are modern reprints of stories that originally appeared in 1901-10 magazines. "Compleat" was not modernized, as it is likely a pun on "The Compleat Angler." This collection is as complete as we can make it, but approximately 14 of these "school" stories appeared only in obscure and rare magazines c.1902–06, and we were unable to find sources.
